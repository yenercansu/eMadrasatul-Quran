
Audio and recitation:
• Access to a wide range of Quran reciters through integrated audio APIs
• Ability to play, pause, and switch between reciters بسهولة
• Option to split ayahs into smaller audio segments for focused memorization
• Ability to loop specific ayah parts or words up to 10x for repetition
Progress tracking:
• Automatic saving of the last listened ayah, allowing users to continue from the same point next time
• Main screen displays recently accessed ayahs for quick return
• Resume feature available directly on the home screen, on the tile showing recent ayahs, allowing users to continue exactly where they stopped without entering the ayah view
Tafsir and content layers:
• Integration of multiple Tafsir sources within the app
• Toggle to show or hide Tafsir
• Toggle to show or hide translations
• Toggle to show or hide transliterations
• All toggles off enables a clean reading mode with minimal distractions
• Option to read in Mushaf style as well
Word level interaction:
• Press and hold any word to instantly view its meaning
• Word by word translation available on interaction, displayed as a pop up; the pop up includes the translation and options to “add to library” and “highlight the word”
• Ability to tap or drag words to save them into a personal vocabulary library
Vocabulary and quiz system:
• Personal word library built from saved words
• Quiz mode that tests the meanings of saved words
• Drag and drop interaction to add words into the quiz system
• Clear and immediate feedback during quizzes to reinforce learning
How ayahs are shown:
• Each page displays 10 ayahs 
Memorization support:
• Ability to highlight difficult words for focused practice; long press on a word to apply highlight, long press again to remove it
• Practice mode that isolates selected highlighted words or specific ayah segments for targeted memorization
Gamification:
• After adding words to the library, users can enter a game mode from within the library
• In the game, users are either asked for the meanings of words in Turkish or presented with an ayah where a previously saved word is removed
• The removed word is replaced with multiple choice options, and users must select the correct word from the given choices
• If a user likes a specific test, they can generate a temporary link and share it with a friend; the link expires once the test is closed to prevent unnecessary database load
Customization and visual tools:
• Color coding system that links Arabic words with their translation and transliteration
• Toggle to turn color coding on or off
• Tajweed color coding toggle for pronunciation guidance
• Automatic rule: when translation color coding is active, Tajweed color coding is turned off to avoid visual overload
